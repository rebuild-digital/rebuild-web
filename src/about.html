{{ title }}

Rebuild is a catalyst to rebuild European social platforms as an industry. Gathering the people in Europe who can make it happen. The entrepreneurs, the pioneers, the investors, the digital leaders.

As a catalyst, Rebuild is here to get things started and move things forward, and is intentionally designed to close down exactly one year from start. Making space for the entrepreneurs and the social platforms themselves to own the initiatives that should shape the industry for the next decade.

On 15 December 2026, Rebuild will host the last gathering in Paris, open-sourcing all frameworks, tools and resources back to the industry.

Rebuild is an NGO, registered in Denmark, supported and funded by European entrepreneurs.

Founded by Thomas Madsen Mygdal, patroned by Margrethe Vestager.
