What are Rebuild gatherings?

Rebuild gatherings are intensive three-day convening of Europe's social platform builders, pioneers, investors, and thinkers. Held three times across 2026, each gathering is designed as a catalyst — bringing the right people into the same room to accelerate what cannot happen at a distance.

Unlike traditional conferences, Rebuild gatherings are built around work. Participants come to build, not to watch. Sessions are hands-on: designing products, solving shared challenges, prototyping outcomes, and planning collective action. Every gathering produces tangible results that carry the industry forward.

The three gatherings form a deliberate arc. Copenhagen is the beginning — a first meeting, a shared diagnosis, and the start of collaboration. Helsinki is the boost — putting programmes in place, deepening craft, and preparing for the Big Shift Day. Paris is the shift — mobilising Europe, celebrating breakthroughs, and open-sourcing everything back to the industry.

Participation is by invitation. If you are building a social platform in Europe, supporting those who are, or pioneering what comes next, you belong in the room.
